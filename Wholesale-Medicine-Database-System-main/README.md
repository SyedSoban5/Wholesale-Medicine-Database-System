# Wholesale Medicine Database System

## 📌 Project Overview
The Wholesale Medicine Database System is a real-world database project developed for a wholesale medicine distribution business. The system is designed to manage medicines, suppliers, customers, orders, and inventory efficiently.

This project helps in organizing pharmaceutical business operations by maintaining accurate records of medicines, monitoring stock levels, tracking expiry dates, and managing customer orders.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Efficient management of medicine data
- Maintain data consistency and integrity
- Track inventory and stock levels
- Monitor medicine expiry dates
- Manage customer orders effectively
- Generate useful business reports

## 📂 Project Modules
### 1. Supplier Management
Stores information about pharmaceutical companies and suppliers.

### 2. Medicine Management
Manages medicine details such as:
- Medicine Name
- Brand
- Category
- Price
- Expiry Date
- Batch Number

### 3. Customer Management
Stores customer information including pharmacies and medical stores.

### 4. Order Management
Maintains records of customer orders and purchased medicines.

### 5. Inventory Management
Tracks medicine stock quantity and minimum stock levels.

---

## 🗂 Database Entities
- Supplier
- Medicines
- Customers
- Orders
- OrderDetails
- Inventory

---

## 🔗 Relationships
- One Supplier supplies many Medicines (1:M)
- One Customer places many Orders (1:M)
- One Order contains multiple Medicines (M:N)
- One Medicine can appear in multiple Orders
- One Medicine has one Inventory record (1:1)
